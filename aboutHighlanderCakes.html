About Highlander Cakes

Highlander Cakes is owned and operated by Robin Heath.

Robin started baking when she was a child; always creating fun and interesting desserts for her family.

After having kids she decided to start baking professionally.

Over the past 25 years her business has grown from doing small cakes for family and friends to now

being able to create cakes and more for anyone in the Albuquerque area.

Please use the form on the Contact Us page to submit questions about an order.

All baking is done wearing masks and gloves for your protection. I have been Covid 19 vaccinated.

I look forward to hearing from you!
